About me

Shooting for the love of it — and sometimes for yours too.

I'm Akash Ravindran. I picked up a camera and never really put it down. Photography is first a hobby, but one I take seriously.

The travel albums — Rome, Venice, Switzerland, London — are personal. Shot because I was there and couldn't not. For clients, I focus on portraits that feel like the person and automotive shoots that do justice to the machine.

Natural light. Honest moments. No overthinking.
